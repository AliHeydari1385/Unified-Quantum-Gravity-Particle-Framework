### Extension to Neutrinos in the Proton-Photon Model

#### 1. **Neutrino Field Equations**
The neutrino field \(\Psi_\nu\) is incorporated through a modified Dirac equation coupled to the proton BEC and photon field:

```latex
\begin{equation}
i\hbar\gamma^\mu D_\mu \Psi_\nu = \left( m_\nu c + g_{\nu p} |\Psi_p|^2 + \lambda_\nu F_{\mu\nu}\sigma^{\mu\nu} \right) \Psi_\nu
\end{equation}
```
where:
- \(g_{\nu p} = \frac{G_F m_p^2}{\sqrt{2}}\) (Fermi coupling)
- \(\lambda_\nu \sim 10^{-11}\mu_B\) (neutrino magnetic moment)
- \(D_\mu = \partial_\mu - iq_\nu A_\mu\) (covariant derivative)

#### 2. **Modified Friedmann Equations**
The cosmological equations are extended to include neutrino contributions:

```latex
\begin{align}
\left(\frac{\dot{a}}{a}\right)^2 &= \frac{8\pi G}{3}\left( \rho_p + \rho_\gamma + \rho_\nu + \rho_{\text{int}} \right) \\
\rho_{\text{int}} &= \alpha\sqrt{\rho_p\rho_\gamma} + \beta\sqrt{\rho_p\rho_\nu} + \gamma\rho_\nu^{3/2}
\end{align}
```
